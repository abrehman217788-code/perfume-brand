Tokyo / Underground

We view garments not as fashion, but as mobile architecture. Every seam, every textile choice, and every silhouette is engineered for the modern urban environment. Our process rejects traditional softness in favor of unapologetic utility.

The streets demand resilience. We construct gear that acts as a barrier and an amplifier. Heavyweight canvases, rigid hardware, and high-contrast visuals define our visual language. We don't follow seasons; we issue drops when the prototypes pass our brutal testing protocols.
